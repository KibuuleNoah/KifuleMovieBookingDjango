At what time are the movies shown?

Every movie that is on a given day is shown thrice that day in our different halls in the morning, afternoon and evening.
Thank you!

How do i know in which hall is my movie to be shown?

We have your hall printed on the ticket given out.
We also pin different halls on our notice board.
Thank you!

What if i pay for a movie and i fail to come to watch it.

The ticket is a oneday ticket, you can watch the movie at the day it is set to be.
If you miss the morning period, you can go for the afternoon or evening.
For the next day, your ticket is void.
Thank you!

How can i pay?

We have two modes of payments, there is Cash on entrance (COD) and online payment using Razorpay.
Here you can use your debit card.
Thank you!

I get different amount when i try paying online, why?

Our payment system converts your total amount to another currency that the system understands, if you continue, the original amount is to be deducted from your account.Though this is yet to be fixed.
Thank you!

Can i pay using mobile money?

For online payments, you can not use mobile money yet but you can use your mobile money line to create a mastercard that is used for payments.
Thank you!
